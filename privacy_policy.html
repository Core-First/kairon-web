Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Kairon ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by Kairon.

2. Information Collection

Local Data Storage: Kairon stores all timetable data, class schedules, and personal settings locally on your device using a secure SQLite database. The AI Assistant processes your data on-device to provide intelligent responses while maintaining complete privacy.

OCR Processing: All image and PDF processing for timetable extraction happens locally on your device using on-device OCR technology. No images or documents are uploaded to our servers.

3. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including biometric authentication (fingerprint/face recognition) and secure local storage. Since your data never leaves your device, you are in full control of your information.

4. Permissions

Kairon requires certain permissions to function correctly:

  • • Camera/Storage: To upload timetable images or PDFs.
  • • Biometrics: To secure the app.
  • • Notifications: To send class reminders.
